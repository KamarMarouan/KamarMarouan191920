Schnippers

Hot Chicken Please!

After a food filled weekend in Nashville, the Schnipper brothers knew they had to bring authentic southern style hot chicken to New York. So the duo scoured the most famous spice shops in Manhattan and set out to develop their very own recipes for their first delivery only service—Jon & Andy’s Hot Chicken. The menu is simple: choose from tenders, sandwiches or wings, tossed with our proprietary spice blend, prepared to your preferred heat level. Add one of our best selling sides and you have yourself one finger licking good meal you’ll want to devour every day.
